Materials Matter

We carefully select every material to minimize environmental impact without compromising performance.

Recycled Polyester

Made from 100% post-consumer plastic bottles

Each pair of Muse Leggings diverts 12 plastic bottles from oceans and landfills. Recycled polyester uses 59% less energy and produces 32% less CO2 than virgin polyester.

Organic Cotton

GOTS Certified Organic

Grown without pesticides or synthetic fertilizers, organic cotton uses 91% less water than conventional cotton and supports healthier soil and farming communities.

TENCEL™ Lyocell

From sustainably managed forests

Made from wood pulp using a closed-loop process that recycles 99% of water and solvents. Biodegradable and exceptionally soft.

Eco-Dye Technology

Waterless dyeing process

Our partnership with DyeCoo enables waterless dyeing using recycled CO2. No wastewater, no chemicals, zero pollution.

2
