Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days within the US. Express shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout. International shipping times vary by location.

What's your return policy?

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached. Email support@verdantmuse.com to initiate a return.

Are your products truly sustainable?

Yes! We use 100% recycled or organic materials, carbon-neutral shipping, and partner with ethical factories. We're B Corp certified and donate 1% to environmental causes.

How do I find my size?

Check our detailed size guide on each product page. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit. Contact us for personalized sizing help!

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Customs fees may apply for international orders.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. Contact us immediately with your order number and we'll help you out.
